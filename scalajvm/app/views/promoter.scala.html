The Promoter symbol can be aligned to the backbone in four modes: backbone-centred, strand-relative, above-strand and below-strand. The strand-centred CDS is centred on the backbone line. The strand-relative Promoter is placed above the backbone for forward-direction symbols, and under the backbone for reverse-direction symbols. For above-strand and below-strand, it is always placed that side. The outside label is always placed furthest from the strand. The arrow always points to the right for forward-strand and to the left for backward-strand glyphs.
